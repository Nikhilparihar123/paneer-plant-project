About Dairy Fresh

Delivering fresh, natural, and high-quality dairy products since 1980.

Our Journey

Started as a small family-owned dairy plant in 1980, Dairy Fresh has grown into one of the most trusted names in Agra's dairy industry. Over the decades, we have maintained our commitment to quality, freshness, and customer satisfaction.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide every household with pure, fresh, and healthy dairy products, directly from our plant to your doorstep.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted dairy brand across India by maintaining quality, innovation, and sustainability in every product.

Why Choose Us?
